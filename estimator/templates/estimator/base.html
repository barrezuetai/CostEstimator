What is Pricestimator?

Pricestimator is a tool developed by Isaac Barrezueta using the algorithm found in K's study. By entering the hospital's annual financial records, we can extract their overall revenue and their deficit and compare it with data found in the ORM in order to come up with a Price Cost Ratio. Using that ratio, we can estimate procedure prices for a hospital that's about 80% correct.
